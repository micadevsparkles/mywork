This POS system was developed for a local clothing store to streamline inventory management, product tracking, employee scheduling, and sales registration. It integrates the Brazilian instant payment system Pix, allowing customers to complete transactions in real time through QR code generation. The system is directly connected to Google Sheets, enabling the store owner to manage and edit data outside the main interface, as well as export or analyze information. Additionally, employees can generate and download sales reports in PDF format directly from the platform.
